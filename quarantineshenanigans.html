What is this?

Myself and my crew trying to make the most out of our time, safely. This spans to trying new things, supporting each other, and doing our best to stay motivated.



A Toast To Making Efforts

This page is a compilation of activities that I have been up to or accomplished during quarantine, along with my family and friends, to keep motivated and stay productive through different means during the COVID-19 global pandemic. I believe it is a great accomplishment in itself to stay positive and if possible, be able to spread that positivity or inspire others when times are tough.


3D Printing PPE

During my time with Universal Creative, I collaborated with the Universal Parks Technology Team as the primary efforts at Universal Orlando Resort to 3D print and deliver PPE for Central Florida frontline healthcare workers including making Face Shields, Ear Guards, and more. I led a team of 8 3D printers and drivers to organize prints, 3D filament, and delivery routes to produce dozens of PPE each week over the course of 2 months at the beginning of stay-at-home orders in Central Florida.

Check out a compilation video here.


Semi-Virtual GTMK 2020 Game Jam

My friends and I participated in the Game Makers Tool Kit Game Jam this year. While some of us participated in the game jam together physically, others worked with us remotely and across timezones as well. We used Discord as our main communication platform and developed our final submission called 'Oh My God!'. Check it out!


Moving Cross-Country

Unfortunately, my role with Universal was eliminated due to COVID-19. I'll miss the crazy awesomeness that was creating world-class location-based attractions, but I knew I gained a lot of experiences from my career that would be put to good use in my next adventure! After collecting myself, within a month's time I created a covid-safe plan, packed my car, and made the roadtrip from Florida to California to pursue my passion in product and user experience back on the West Coast where I originally came from.


Design Portfolio Makeover

With new dreams to make come true, I took the time to completely re-do my design portfolio. Using my fundamental knowledge in HTML and CSS, I put together up this site using a UI tool kit. I have had quite a lot of fun updating the content as I continue to gather feedback while I pursue my job hunt!


Cafe Quarantine

Who hasn't upped their baking game during quarantine? Myself, I want to improve on my frosting piping skills! I also tried recreating some of the popular cafe drinks at home that are making circles online.


Get Your Blood Cycling

My friends and I started a virtual fitness group that gets together twice a week to do high intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts. The group served as great motivation to keep exercising even if we couldn't attend sessions in person. Our favorite workouts come from Chloe Ting and Pamela Reif. I also enjoy running through the neighborhood and going on socially distanced hikes.


New Gaming Things

I finally bought my own Nintendo Switch this year and was swept up in the Animal Crossing: New Horizons wave! My friends and I also enjoyed Jackbox Party Game Nights together. It's been a while since I've played religiously, but I'm also determined to finally hit Level 40 (currently lvl 38) in Pokémon Go! I'm looking forward to Nintendo's new Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity coming out in November.
