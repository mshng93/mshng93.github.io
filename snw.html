Super Nintendo World™

Associate Interactives Designer - Land


Confidentiality Notice: As certain features for this land have not yet been publicized, I am unable to disclose certain details of my work for the time being.

A REAL-LIFE VIDEO GAME

Super Nintendo World™ is a life-sized living video game with a whole new level of play that is unlike any other theme park. This is possible with the new wearable 'Power Up Band' that is linked to guest's Smartphone, allowing them to connect to elements in the land in a seamless way. The app will keep track of items collected in the land such as coins and stamps similar to items collected in Nintendo® video games.

AN INTERACTIVE LAND

I worked on a multidisciplinary team comprised of producers, designers, developers, and vendors to design and commission interactive experiences for the Super Nintendo World™ theme park land coming soon to Universal Studios Japan and other Universal Parks worldwide. I was primarily involved with the mobile component of the land's gameplay.

MOBILE DESIGN

I participated in several brainstorm and design iteration sessions to define features that should be present in the mobile experience. I also created initial user flow diagrams and wireframes of the app's layout. Once the app was in production with our vendor, I helped to provide feedback on the product's design. The team also received great feedback from working with Nintendo® to drive decisions on various features.

At certain occasions, I would also be present to help demonstrate the app's capabilities and use debug tools for testing purposes.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

At the beginning of the project, I helped in the vendor contracting process to kick-off app development. I then transitioned into a project management role following deliverable of the app's verticle slice. I coordinated efforts between our project team and our vendors to ensure production was on schedule. I communicated with various stakeholders of the project such as our vendors, the Universal Studios Japan Project Team (who also communicated with our IP), Universal's Marketing Team, and Universal's Parks Technology Team.

UNIVERSAL CREATIVE TOOLS

I created the UX/UI and interaction design of an internal tool used for the test and adjust of a major experience coming soon to Super Nintendo World™. See an overview of my design tools process here.
