Confidentiality Notice: I am unable to disclose certain aspects of this project as it serves many products that are still under development.


GROWING A BRAND

The Advanced Technology & Interactives (ATI) Team was a small, multi-faceted group with most of its members physically deployed to various projects. The sole goal I was presented was to create a presentation title slide that could be used by any ATI Team Member when presenting their work. My process and work on this initial asset was well received and grew into a larger overall visual brand for the team.


VISUAL DESIGN

I worked closely with the team's Creative Director to understand what the general look and feel should be like. I created several iterations of assets and color combinations before beginning on the actual slide design. This process lent itself nicely to when I finally created the images that would eventually be folded into the themes of various presentation platforms such as PowerPoint and Keynote.

This is a higher level look at the process of creating the title slide, taking both elements of our team name and inspiration from "technology" patterns.


I followed up by producing the following team-branded templates and assets.

  • Microsoft Powerpoint Assets and Theme (Master Slides)
  • Microsoft Word Cover Page and Theme
  • Keynote Theme (Master Slides)
  • Custom posters
  • Internal TV "ads" around the office
  • Event signage

    • USE CASES

    I also created many assets for various showcases of our team's work when it is better to demonstrate a prototype or technology to Team Members in person. These assets included description signage, email invites, presentations, notices, and more. We sometimes bring in over 100 total Team Members depending on what or how many projects are being showcased or user tested.

    I often made custom presentations for ATI leadership to present to Universal Creative stakeholders. These presentations ranged from project updates, financial planning, and more. Depending on the use case, I would sometime also create custom infographics in order to best portray the information being communicated.

    BRANDING GROWTH

    My work, along with a few other branding pioneers at Universal Creative, became part of a larger initiative for reimagining Universal Creative's overall brand. A year after I entered the company, I got to witness Universal Creative form it's own Branding and Communications's team and was lucky enough to take part in an early consultation panel while the group was developing how to communicate Universal Creative to the world.
