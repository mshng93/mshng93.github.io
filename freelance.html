Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, InVision, Shopify




MY ROLE

I contracted with Advocado to help streamline a few of their client eCommerce webpages using a user experience design approach. The primary client I worked with sold all-natural hair and skin products. During my time with Advocado, I sketched different wireframes for several of the eCommerce pages, discussed branding elements with the Advocado CEO, applied changes to the client Shopify site, looked at potential Shopify plug-ins, and imported quick mockups into InVision for developer reference.

Below is a working brand style guide I created for the client. I created test SKUs to test typography, colors, buttons, and other elements in a separate copy of the client's Shopify webpage. I also suggested different art style approaches while working with the client's product images.




REFLECTION

My work with Advocado and their clients was an enriching experience. I learned about client relations and working to understand their business values. In the end, the designs I worked with were not implemented as we required more time with the client to convince them to streamline their product communication. Nonetheless, I was excited to work on designs that were on route to be developed and published.
