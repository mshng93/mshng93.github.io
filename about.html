The Adventure Begins

My journey into UX began as a student at Simon Fraser University (Canada) where my passion to create things for people to enjoy crossed paths with my interest in design and technology.

While I was working in the Universal Creative division at the Universal Parks & Resorts headquarters in Orlando, I developed a product management mindset required to create experiences that can last or be updated for years to come. I was lucky to have been able to work with some amazing intellectual properties such as Nintendo, Harry Potter, Jurassic World, and Fast & Furious.



Are We A Good Fit?

I thrive in teams that value a customer-first approach, uses data-driven design methods, whose creativity stems from having diverse perspectives, and encourages learning. I enjoy pulling inspiration from the broader entertainment industry including music and video games.
