    • I strive to design and continue educating myself to design with these philosophies in mind to make better experiences for people:

    Accessibility: Strive to use practices that make experiences more inclusive for different people with different needs. This way, the products we make can reach a larger audience internationally.

    Intuitiveness: Remove pain points so the focal content and storyline can stand out. Creating a smaller learning curve can help to achieve higher adoption and retention.

    Delight: Experiences should be fun! Delightful bouts of joy sprinkled throughout the design can augment the experience, but should not take away from the core functionality. This is a balancing act.
