BRIEF

Design and development of a Fast & Furious tablet game to be implemented at the Fast & Furious: Supercharged attraction Childswap Room.

FAST & FURIOUS CHILDSWAP ROOM

Universal Orlando Resort™ Childswap Rooms are holding areas for families with small children or those who are unable to ride the attraction without needing to exit the queue. Besides seating, Childswap Rooms do not have much to offer for those who most frequently occupy this space.

The games in the Fast & Furious: Supercharged Childswap Room are the first of its kind to be implemented at Universal Orlando Resort™.

TABLET UX DESIGN

I collaborated closely with an artist and a developer to create the UX of the proposed tablet mini-games. The 3 mini-games on the tablet were a matching game, coloring game, and puzzle game. The group worked to determine design considerations for the target audience of children of 3-6 years old such as ability to understand the experience with minimal text. Our process included whiteboarding and creating mockups before handing off to development.

Some of the other considerations included were:

  • Larger buttons and hit-boxes
  • Game reset after idle period
  • Limiting choices to streamline arriving at gameplay
  • Maximizing gameplay display area


    • Safe to say the F&F Childswap Room is cooler than some of the others out there!



    Childswap Rooms are a lower priority item when designing attractions. It was very rewarding to have been part of designing an experience allowing for a wider variety of guests to enjoy and access the intellectual property who are unable to ride. I hope the company considers this more often when designing future attractions.
