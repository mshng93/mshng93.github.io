Associate Interactives Designer - Land


Confidentiality Notice: As certain features for this land have not yet been publicized, I am unable to disclose certain details of my work for the time being.

A REAL-LIFE VIDEO GAME

Super Nintendo World™ is a life-sized living video game with a whole new level of play that is unlike any other theme park. This is possible with the new wearable 'Power Up Band' that is linked to guest's Smartphone, allowing them to connect to elements in the land in a seamless way. The app will keep track of items collected in the land such as coins and stamps similar to items collected in Nintendo® video games.

My Role

I worked on a multidisciplinary team comprised of producers, designers, developers, and vendors to design and commission interactive experiences for the Super Nintendo World™ theme park land coming soon to Universal Studios Japan and other Universal Parks worldwide. Some of my tasks involved:

  • Assisting in the vendor contracting process
  • Vendor management and stakeholder communication
  • Creating guest flow diagrams, wireframes, and initial mobile interface mockups
  • Working with the Interactives Team to iterate on the product designs
  • Producing UX, UI, and interactions of internal tools used for the test and adjust of both prototypes and guest-facing experiences
