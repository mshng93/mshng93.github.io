Access is a mobile app paired with an online platform which allows American Express cardholders to access a premium tier of their favourite events and experiences.






This app was designed to be an extension of the existing American Express 'Membership Experiences' program. Targeted towards the more affluent consumer, Access recommends premium events for users based on their purchase history. This creates a personalized membership experience, redefining what it is like to be an American Express cardholder.


MY ROLE

I put on multiple hats for this project as project manager, but I was primarily responsible for the app’s user experience design. With the help of personas, I created the app's UI flow diagram, produced wireframes, and made the experience journey map. This created the foundation for the interface, interaction, and visual designers.




The experience journey map below helps to emphasize the app's touchpoints from both consumer and the company's point of view and was used to determine design decisions.




DESIGN THINKING - BUSINESS PROBLEM

The value of being an AMEX member was facing competition as spend-based benefits, such as cash-back rewards, have become a standard in financial services industry. Thus, there was no incentive to renew or sign up for an AMEX Membership.

To differentiate American Express from their competitors, we strived to increase American Express's presence in the technology sector by highlighting the 'Member Experiences' program and creating a more personalized, memorable experience for AMEX members.




DESICION MAKING

Access includes features such as a recommendation list, bookmarking, and filters. As a project manager, I worked to consolidate our team's several ideas and helped to make end-decisions on what should be implemented into the app based on our scope of work. This included deciding what features the app should entail and the push to create a complementing online platform while keeping the focus on the mobile app.

REFLECTION

Through careful research and analysis, we were able to agree on a client, address a core business problem, and work in the realm of experience design that aligned with our own individual values while staying true to the client’s brand.

My greatest takeaway was integrating a story throughout the entire app. As a project manager, I needed to understand the app through the entire development process and ensured that each decision aligned with our team, the company and their clients in an empathetic way.
