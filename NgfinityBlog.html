MY EXCHANGE TO SEOUL NATIONAL UNIVERSITY

From September 2014 - June 2015, I participated in an international study abroad term to Seoul National University, one of the top educational institutions in Seoul, South Korea. This was my first independent venture outside of Vancouver and to a country I did not understand the native language! Nonetheless, my exchange term was one of the best experiences of my life and had to document every moment possible.

I wrote a blog entry for every week of my exchange for personal documentation, but I wanted to inform and inspire other students to participate in an exchange program as well. My enthusiasm for exchange helped me win a scholarship from the British Columbia Study Abroad (BCSA) Consortium and I received the opportunity to write a short series of blog posts as part of their 'Writers in Residence' column on their website.





Upon my return to Vancouver, I had the pleasure of sharing my experience by speaking at one of my university’s study abroad information sessions and at one of my faculty’s student union general meetings as a spotlight student.
