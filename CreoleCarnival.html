Universal Play™ - Creole Carnival Games

Product Owner



THE BRIEF

Create a live guest-facing product for Universal Orlando Resort's Mardi Gras 25th anniversary event (Feb. 1 - April 2, 2020) in partnership with The Coca-Cola® Company.

THE AIM

  • Release event features to guests outside of the Universal Orlando Resort parks - a first in events history where guests are able to interact with Universal Parks & Resorts event content while not on property.
  • Design the experience to be cohesive with this year's event theme Treasures of the Deep.
  • Integrate with various divisions of Universal Orlando Resort including Entertainment and Food and Beverage.
  • Partner with The Coca-Cola® Company for approval on all aspects of the experience.

    • CREOLE CARNIVAL GAMES

    Creole Carnival Games was a limited time, exclusive Universal Play™ experience that allowed guests to further engage with this year's Mardi Gras 2020 event and immerse themselves into the all-new 'Treasures of the Deep' theme. In partnership with The Coca-Cola® Company, Creole Carnival Games could be unlocked within the Universal Orlando Resort app by scanning the QR code found within specially marked Coca-Cola® and Coca-Cola® Zero Sugar packages in the state of Florida.

    Release

    Coca-Cola® and Coca-Cola® Zero Sugar packaging featuring Universal Orlando's Mardi Gras and Creole Carnival Games was released to Florida Publix grocery shelves on January 7, 2020. All printed packages were sold before the conclusion of the Mardi Gras event. Additional Coca-Cola® sponsored advertisements ran through until Friday, March 13, 2020.

    Product

    I led the design and development of Creole Carnival Games working with both our internal groups at Universal Orlando Resort as well as our vendors.

    The experience featured a Food and Beverage Passport, exclusive UO Mardi Gras story content, 2 mobile mini-games, and achievements earned by scanning QR code "treasures" in-park. As our guests participated in the usual Universal Orlando (UO) Mardi Gras activties such as sampling Louisiana-inspired foods, purchasing event merchandise, and grabbing beads during the parade, they could discover and earn digital treasures along the way. The more you played and unlocked in the game, the more achievements you earned.

    DESIGN & MANAGEMENT

    I coordinated with several stakeholders throughout the run of this product to communicate our creative intent, obtain approvals, and ultimately driving the product to launch.

    We are grateful to The Coca-Cola® Company who was, and continue to be, a great partner to work with and supports our team's ideas.

    COVID-19 PARKS CLOSURE

    Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, Universal Orlando Resort closed their parks to the public on March 15, 2020 through to (at minimum) May 31, 2020. Thus, this concluded the Mardi Gras event early. After discussions with our partners, we decided to keep the Creole Carnival Games experience live on the Universal Orlando Resort App for the original run-time of the event through to April 2, 2020. Although we saw a drop in activity for the game during this time, we were glad to maintain the spirit of our Mardi Gras event running as originally intended.

    METRICS

  • Pre Mardi Gras Runtime: Jan 7, 2020 - Feb 1, 2020
  • Mardi Gras Actual Runtime: Feb 1, 2020 - March 15, 2020
  • Full Game Runtime: Jan 7, 2020 - Apr 2, 2020
  • Total Players: 11,000+
