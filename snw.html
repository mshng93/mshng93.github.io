SUPER NINTENDO WORLD™

Associate Interactives Designer - Land


Summary

I worked on a multidisciplinary team comprised of producers, designers, developers, and vendors to bring together interactive experiences to the Super Nintendo World™ theme park land coming soon to Universal Studios Japan and other Universal Parks worldwide. Some of my tasks involed:

  • Assisting in the vendor contracting process
  • Vendor management and stakeholder communication
  • Working with the Interactives Team to iterate on the product design
  • Producing UX, UI, and interactions of internal tools used for the test and adjust of both prototypes and guest-facing experiences

    • As certain features for this land have not yet been publicized, I am unable to disclose further details of my work for the time being. However, I am happy to share Universal Parks & Resorts' announcements featuring some of Super Nintendo World™'s interactive elements I have had the pleasure of being involved with.
