Universal Play™ - Creole Carnival Games

A Mobile Treasure Hunt Around the Parks

ROLE

Mobile Product Owner, Product Design

BRIEF

Create a live guest-facing product for Universal Orlando Resort™'s Mardi Gras 25th anniversary event (Feb. 1 - April 2, 2020) in partnership with The Coca-Cola® Company.

CREOLE CARNIVAL GAMES

Creole Carnival Games was a limited time, exclusive Universal Play™ experience that allowed guests to further engage with this year's Mardi Gras 2020 event and immerse themselves into the all-new 'Treasures of the Deep' theme.

In partnership with The Coca-Cola® Company, Creole Carnival Games could be unlocked within the Universal Orlando Resort™ app by scanning the QR code found within specially marked Coca-Cola® and Coca-Cola® Zero Sugar packages in the state of Florida.



PRODUCT DESIGN

I led the design and development of Creole Carnival Games in the short span of 2 months before the live event. As Product Owner, I worked with our Advanced Technology, Marketing, Entertainment, Food & Beverage, Legal, and Global Partnerships teams to define the best features to produce for the game on this expedited timeline. As a Product Designer, I worked closely with our vendor, providing diagrams, wireframes, and design feedback while also driving the software development schedule. The final experience featured:

  • Food and Beverage Passport
  • Food & Coca-Cola® Drink Recommendations
  • Exclusive Universal Orando Mardi Gras Story Content
  • 2 Mobile Mini-games: An Endless Runner and a Tile-Matching Game
  • Achievements System


    • The experience drew over 11,000 players over the run of the event!



    Universal Orlando Resort™ released 'Event' content to guests without the need to be on property for the first time ever with the Creole Carnival Games experience.



    Coca-Cola® and Coca-Cola® Zero Sugar packaging featuring Universal Orlando's Mardi Gras and Creole Carnival Games was released to Florida Publix grocery shelves on January 7, 2020.

    All printed packages were SOLD OUT before the conclusion of the Mardi Gras event!
