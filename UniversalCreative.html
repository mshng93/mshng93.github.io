Associate Designer

Tools: Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office Tools, Keynote, Ultimaker and FormLabs 3D Printing

I am currently an Associate Designer with Universal Creative's Advanced Technology Interactives (ATI) and Research and Development team in Orlando, Florida at Universal Orlando Resort. I've had the pleasure of being able to work on several projects designing immersive themed experiences utilizing new technology.

My adventure with Universal Creative began as a Design Intern in September 2016. I transitioned into a Creative Coordinator role before being promoted to an Associate Designer.




MY ROLE

My work is a balance between building a brand for the ATI team, brainstorming and designing future experiences for various projects across the Universal Parks and Resorts portfolios in Orlando, Hollywood, and Japan.

I design wireframes and interfaces for internal tools used for test and adjust of new guest experiences, act as a UX/UI consultant between Universal Creative and Parks Technology, and create mockups to pitch new queue and land interactive ideas for future projects. I provided UX/UI feedback for the tablet childswap game which was implemented in the new Fast & Furious: Supercharged attraction which opened May 2018 at Universal Studios Florida.




I assist in contracting vendors and writing vendor scopes of work for the development and integration of new projects. I manage internal and external communication between project stakeholders to ensure action items are fulfilled for a timely milestone delivery. I also participate in design evaluation of vendor work throughout production.


BRANDING MY TEAM

I designed the brand identity for the ATI team which includes a brand style guide, Microsoft Office and Keynote templates, report structures and other visual representations as resources for the team to use during presentations and documentation of work. Collecting team status was also one of my tasks. I reported directly to the Senior Director of ATI performing information design for exhibits of progress and strategy which have been seen by the President and Vice-Presidents of Universal Creative.


SXSW 2018

A fun project I was a part of was a Universal Creative collaboration with Sony Technologies called ‘Ghostly Whisper’ which was showcased at Sony's WOW Studio warehouse for SXSW 2018. The show combined Universal’s storytelling with Sony’s audio and haptic technology to deliver a simulated paranormal experience to guests. I designed the lighting cues and helped choose props for the set. I also acted as a stagehand who helped run the show backstage when the show was in development in Orlando and at the SXSW Conference for the Interactives Festival.




MANAGING COOL TECH

I process all the 3D printing requests for ATI and requests from other teams within Universal Creative. 3D printers I have worked with include Ultimaker and FormLabs products. I manage the one of Universal Creative’s lab spaces which includes the team's 3D printers and other equipment used for building prototypes and ensuring safety and proper use of equipment is being following by all lab users.
