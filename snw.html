A Real-Life Video Game

Super Nintendo World™ is a life-sized living video game with a whole new level of play that is unlike any other theme park! This is possible with the new wearable 'Power Up Band' that is linked to guest's smartphone, allowing them to connect to elements in the land in a seamless way. The app will keep track of items collected in the land such as coins and stamps similar to items collected in Nintendo® video games.


my role

Mobile Design

I was involved in the concept and design of the smartphone app players will be able to use throughout the parks, including creating user flow diagrams, wireframes, early mockups, providing design feedback, test and adjust. Prior to the Global Kick-off Presentation, I helped to demonstrate the work in progress to key stakeholders.

Project Management

I worked on the smartphone app vendor contract which transitioned into project management. I coordinated efforts between between stakeholders from around the world to assure project schedule.

Internal GUI for Gameplay Testing

My work with the Super Nintendo World™ property also included the production of an internal graphic user interface (GUI) for the project teams to test and adjust, in real-time, the game mechanics of a new interactive attraction coming to the land. I partnered closely with a software developer to take a usability driven approach to designing and executing the GUI targeting for use by our internal teams. Our first release created for Super Nintendo World™ was so successful, the product sparked a desire to expand the development of the GUI into a template to be used for developing attractions in other areas of Universal Parks & Resorts as well.
