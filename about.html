My Story

I am a product and UX designer currently in San Francisco looking for my next big design adventure.

My personal narrative is all about creating meaningful user experiences with an emphasis on storytelling. I enjoy designing for consumer facing cross platform products that bring people together. My design philosophies are to make user experiences intuitive, accessible, and delightful.

I love to travel and immerse myself in new cultures. I draw inspiration from the diversity of people I met having lived in a few different cities around the world such as Vancouver, Seoul, Orlando, and now San Francisco.

My designs have a clean aesthetic as I like to highlight the story or primary content I am presenting through the product such as for video games, entertainment, and controls.
