Involvement

I am glad you decided to pop into this page! I am very proud of my volunteer and extracurricular involvement and would love to share some of my experiences with you.


ORLANDO

I was very happy to learn that Universal Orlando offers several volunteering opportunities within the Orlando community. During my time with Universal Creative, I have volunteered for Second Harvest Food Bank, Keep Orlando Beautiful, and Habitat for Humanity of Greater Orlando.


GLOBAL ECO DESIGNER

During my time on international exchange at Seoul National University, I volunteered internationally with Hyundai Hysco and Better World Foundation as a 'Global Eco Designer' to teach elementary school children in rural cities about sustainability issues. In addition to my blog posts about my university life in Seoul, I documented each of our sessions with the students.
