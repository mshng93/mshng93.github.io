Universal Creative Design Intern

I am a design intern with Universal Creative's Advanced Technology Interactives team at the Universal Orlando Resort. I've had the pleasure of being able to work on several projects utilizing technology specifically for the themed entertainment industry.

Throughout my time with Universal Creative, I have

  • Designed wireframes and high fidelity mobile interfaces to enhance guest experiences in attraction queues and around the theme parks
  • Ideated and proposed integration of new mobile features to increase revenue and guest visitation to the resort
  • Produced and streamlined user journey maps for the design and development of large-scale themed lands
  • Conducted technology previews for Universal Creative Team Members to drive innovation and inspiration to create new theme park experiences
